About Us

Who We Are

Policy Hub Business Facilitators EST. is a leading Corporate Services Provider (CSP) in Dubai, dedicated to simplifying the complexities of doing business in the United Arab Emirates. As a licensed Sole Establishment, we provide efficient, reliable, and personalized solutions that help businesses navigate the UAE’s business landscape with ease.

Led by founder Husain Rayammarakkar Veettil Hassan Hassan, our experienced team combines deep local knowledge with a commitment to exceptional customer service, ensuring your success and peace of mind.
